Creator Agreement — Multi-Risk Partnership

Brand: TrendWave Fashion
Agency: MediaFirst Partners
Creator: Jordan Lee

Compensation
Brand shall pay Creator $6,000 on Net 60 terms following receipt of invoice. Payment will be processed through Agency's standard accounts payable cycle.

Deliverables
Creator shall produce content as requested by Brand. Specific deliverables, platforms, and format requirements are to be agreed upon and subject to Brand's approval process.

Usage Rights
Brand is granted perpetual, worldwide rights to use, reproduce, and distribute all content created under this agreement. Creator grants Brand the right to use content for paid social advertising, whitelisting, and digital ad placements across all platforms in perpetuity. Brand may sublicense content to retail partners for in-store displays and promotional materials.

Exclusivity
Creator agrees to a 90-day category exclusivity period for fashion and apparel brands beginning on the effective date. During this period, Creator shall not promote any competing fashion brand.

Termination
Brand may terminate immediately without cause at its sole discretion. In the event of termination, no further payments shall be owed for uncompleted work.

Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.
