INFLUENCER SERVICES AGREEMENT

Brand: Nimbus Athletics
Creator: Demo Creator
Campaign: Spring Recovery Drop

Compensation. Brand will pay Creator $2,000 for the campaign deliverables. Payment will be made on Net 45 terms following receipt of invoice.

Deliverables. Creator will provide 1 Instagram Reel and 2 Instagram Stories. The Instagram Reel is due March 30, 2026. Stories are due March 31, 2026.

Usage Rights. Brand may repost the content organically across owned social channels. Brand may also use the content in paid social advertising for 6 months from first posting date.

Exclusivity. Creator agrees not to promote competing athletic footwear brands for 30 days following the final post.

Revisions. Brand is entitled to one round of revisions on the draft Reel concept.

Termination. Brand may terminate this Agreement without cause before Creator submits final content, provided Brand pays for approved work completed through the termination date.

Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of New York.
