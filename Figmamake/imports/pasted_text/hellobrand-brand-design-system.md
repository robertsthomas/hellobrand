Design a complete brand identity and product design system for a web app called HelloBrand.

Product:
HelloBrand is an AI contract assistant for creators and influencers. It helps solo creators upload sponsorship contracts, understand them in plain English, extract payment terms and deliverables, flag risky clauses, and generate polished negotiation emails to brands. It is not a law firm and should not feel cold, corporate, or intimidating. It should feel creator-first, premium, modern, calm, trustworthy, and slightly editorial.

Audience:
Solo creators, influencers, YouTubers, TikTok creators, Instagram creators, streamers, newsletter creators. They are creative, busy, not legal experts, and want confidence when reviewing brand deals.

Brand goals:
- Make legal/contract workflows feel simple and empowering
- Feel premium enough to justify paid SaaS pricing
- Feel creator-native, not like enterprise legal software
- Communicate clarity, confidence, polish, and momentum
- Avoid generic “AI SaaS” styling

Visual direction:
- Sophisticated editorial-meets-product aesthetic
- Warm neutrals with one strong deep accent color and one bright highlight color
- Clean layouts, strong typography, generous spacing, rounded panels, subtle texture or gradients
- Premium but approachable
- Avoid purple-heavy AI gradients, crypto vibes, dark-mode-first aesthetics, or generic B2B blues
- Create a distinct visual identity with logo direction, color palette, typography pairings, spacing scale, card styles, buttons, form fields, table styles, icons, and illustration/art direction
- Include both desktop-first designs and mobile-responsive adaptations
