# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-16*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Understand your brand deals in seconds.
**What it does:** Upload a brand partnership contract and get a plain-English breakdown with risk flags, deliverables, and payment terms before you sign. Save deals into workspaces to track follow-up, emails, and payouts in one place.
**Product category:** Creator tools, contract analysis, partnership management
**Product type:** SaaS (freemium)
**Business model:** Free tier (1 partnership), Basic ($29/mo), Premium ($79/mo), annual discounts available

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Solo creators, influencer teams, creator managers, small talent agencies
**Decision-makers:** The creator themselves (solo), creator managers (small rosters)
**Primary use case:** Creators who receive brand deal contracts and want to understand the terms before signing — without paying a lawyer.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Understand what a brand deal contract is actually asking for
- Catch risky clauses (usage rights, payment gaps, vague terms) before committing
- Track deliverables, payments, and brand communications across multiple deals
**Use cases:**
- First-time creators reviewing their first brand deal
- Established creators managing 5+ concurrent partnerships
- Creator managers overseeing a roster of talent
- Anyone who has been burned by bad contract terms in the past

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Creators sign brand contracts they don't fully understand, then lose track of deliverables, deadlines, and payments across multiple deals.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Lawyers cost $200-500/hr — too expensive for a single brand deal
- Spreadsheets don't flag risks or parse contract language
- Generic contract tools aren't built for creator-specific terms (usage rights, content deliverables, net-60 payment timelines)
- Nothing ties the contract to the ongoing brand relationship (emails, follow-ups, payout tracking)
**What it costs them:**
- Missed or vague payment terms = thousands in unpaid or late revenue
- Perpetual usage rights = content used forever without additional compensation
- Scattered tracking = missed deliverables, damaged brand relationships
**Emotional tension:** Anxiety about signing something they don't understand, frustration from chasing payments, overwhelm from managing multiple deals across tools

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Creator contract tools (e.g., CreatorContract, Breeze) — often template-focused, not AI-powered analysis
**Secondary:** General contract analysis (e.g., LegalSift, LawGeex) — not tailored to creator/brand deal terms
**Indirect:** Hiring a lawyer, using spreadsheets, relying on an agent/manager — expensive, manual, or inaccessible for smaller creators

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Free instant contract breakdown with no signup required (anonymous upload)
- Creator-specific risk detection (usage rights, payment timing, content ownership)
- Partnership workspace that ties contract terms to emails, deliverables, and payments
- AI-powered plain-English summary (not legal jargon)
**How we do it differently:** Upload-first, signup-second. The value happens before the account creation, so creators trust the product before committing.
**Why that's better:** Removes friction. Creators see real value in 10 seconds, then choose to save when it matters.
**Why customers choose us:** Zero cost to start, no legal knowledge needed, built specifically for creator-brand deals (not generic contracts).
